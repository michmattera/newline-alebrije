What is an Alebrije?

An alebrije is a mix of 2 or more animals in one. They can be find just in the realm of death and can be brought into our world just in the day of the death ( the 2nd of November).
They also have to match with a person before passing to the other world, otherwise they would be lost in that world forever.
They can be divided in different categories:

  • Water
  • Fire
  • Earth
  • Air
Each one has different powers, but they all have in common their purpose ( Helping the person that they match with and guide it in life).
After you complete the form you will know which kind of alebrije you are going to get.
But if you want more info just keep reading below.
