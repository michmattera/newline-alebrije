What is an Alebrije?

An alebrije is a mix of 2 or more animals in one. They can be found just in the realm of death and can be brought into our world just on the day of death ( the 2nd of November).
They also have to match with a person before passing to the other world, otherwise, they would be lost in that world forever.
They can be divided into different categories:

  • Water
  • Fire
  • Earth
  • Air
Each one has different powers, but they all have in common their purpose ( Helping the person that they match with and guide them in life).
After you complete the form you will know which kind of alebrije you are going to get.
But if you want more information just keep reading below.

Water Alebrije


Pro :

  • Alebrije are the most emotional alebrijes.
  • Their power is to help deal with feelings and not get too overwhelmed by them.
  • They are very emphatic, and very spiritual.
  • They often will come near you to offer help or to see how you are feeling.
  • They like to talk about everything but especially feelings or spiriual things.
  • They are also very smart and probably have the answer for everything .
  • They can manipolate water.
  • Will treat you as a family member.

Con :
  • Not too focused on tasks or jobs.

Fire Alebrije


Pro :

  • Fire Alebrije's are very hard worker.
  • They will help you do all the tasks that you have to do and get whatever you want or need to achieve.
  • They are very impulsive, so they will always say what they have in their mind.
  • They act first and then think.
  • They are very practical.
  • They can manipulate fire.
  • Will treat you as a teammate.

Con :
  • They are not very good with emotions, so they can't help you deal with your feelings.

Earth Alebrije


Pro :

  • Earth's alebrije are very creative.
  • They are loyal and very emotional, even if they do not show it.
  • You will never get bored with them because they are very energetic.
  • They will be always by your side.
  • They will always help you to try to find a compromise and resolve any problem or any discussions.
  • They can manipolate earth.
  • They forgive easily.
  • Will treat you as a brother/sister.

Con :
  • They can be very stubborn and materialistic.

Air Alebrije


Pro :

  • They are the most knowledgeable alebrijes.
  • They often also like to change, and never be in the same place or doing the same things.
  • They are very emotional, and at the same moment very spiritual.
  • They are very curious.
  • They will help you discover the world and all the wonders in it.
  • They can manipolate air.
  • Will treat you as a travel companion and a friend.

Con :
  • They need a lot of motivation and stimulus.
