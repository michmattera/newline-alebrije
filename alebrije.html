Alebrije's

Alebrije's are magical animals . They are spiritual guide that help the person they match with, since the first moment until their human pass to the realm of the death!

What is an Alebrije?

An alebrije is a mix of 2 or more animals in one. They can be find just in the realm of death and can be brought into our world just in the day of the death ( the 1st of November).
They also have to match with a person before passing to the other world, otherwise they would be lost in that world forever.
They can be divided in different categories:

  • Water
  • Fire
  • Air
  • Earth
Each one has different powers, but they all have in common their purpose ( Helping the person that they match with and guide it in life).

Water Alebrije


Pro :

  • Alebrije are the most emotional alebrijes.
  • Their power is to help dealing with feelings and not get too overwhelmed by them.
  • They are very emphatic, and very spiritual.
  • They often will come near you to offer help or to see how you are feeling.

  • They are also very smart and probably have the answer for everything .
  • They can manipolate water.

Con :
  • Not too focused regarding tasks or jobs.

Fire Alebrije


Pro :

  • Fire Alebrije's are very hard worker.
